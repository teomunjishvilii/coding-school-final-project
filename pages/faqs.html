Frequently Asked Questions

How can we help you?

What are your operating hours?

Our gym is open 24/7, so you can work out whenever it fits your schedule. Our staff is available from 6 AM to 10 PM daily to assist you.

Do I need to book classes in advance?

Yes, booking in advance is recommended, especially for popular classes like yoga and spin. You can reserve your spot at the front desk.

What types of memberships do you offer?

We offer a range of memberships including monthly, yearly, and pay-as-you-go options. We also have special discounts for students.

What should I do if I lose something at the gym?

If you lose something, please check with our front desk. We have a lost and found, and our staff will do their best to help you recover your items.

Are there locker rooms and showers?

Yes, we provide fully equipped locker rooms with showers, towels, and hairdryers for your convenience.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we offer free parking to all our members right next to the gym entrance.

How do I freeze or cancel my membership?

You can freeze or cancel your membership by visiting our front desk or contacting us through our customer service hotline. We require at least 30 days' notice for cancellations.

Are there any age restrictions for membership?

Members must be at least 16 years old to join the gym. We also offer special teen memberships for those aged 13-15, which require parental consent and supervision during workouts.

How do I sign up for a gym tour?

You can sign up for a gym tour by calling us, or by stopping by in person. Our staff will be happy to show you around and answer any questions.
